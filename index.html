Your Prep Day Strategy

An hour or two on Saturday sets up your whole week. Here's a smart plan:

🥩 Proteins — 30 min

Oven + stovetop
  • Press & marinate 1 block tofu (soy, ginger, garlic)
  • Bake marinated tofu at 400°F for 25 min → store in fridge
  • Portion and freeze fish in dinner-sized bags
  • Move 1–2 portions to fridge to thaw for Mon/Tue

🥬 Greens — 15 min

Wash, dry, store
  • Wash all greens, spin dry, store in damp paper towel in container
  • Chop kale or chard into bite-sized pieces — ready to sauté
  • Prep a big salad base (no dressing) for easy grab

🍚 Grains & Base — 20 min

Hands off cook time
  • Cook a big pot of brown rice or farro
  • Portion into containers — 1 per weeknight
  • Boil soba or rice noodles, toss with sesame oil to prevent sticking

🫙 Sauces — 10 min

Vitamix makes this fast
  • Miso-ginger sauce (miso, ginger, rice vinegar, sesame oil, garlic)
  • Tahini-lemon dressing (tahini, lemon, garlic, water)
  • Store in mason jars — good all week

Your Vitamix Advantage

A high-powered blender changes what "fast" cooking means. Here's how to use it in your routine.

Weeknight Soup — 15 min total

Blended White Bean & Greens

Sauté greens + garlic 5 min. Add white beans + broth. Blend hot in Vitamix → silky, protein-rich soup. Top with lemon + olive oil.

Weeknight Sauce — 3 min

Instant Miso-Ginger Sauce

Blend: 2 tbsp miso, 1 tbsp ginger, 1 tbsp rice vinegar, 1 tsp sesame oil, 1 garlic clove, 3 tbsp water. Pour over anything. Done.

Morning Boost — 5 min

Green Power Smoothie

Blend: handful of greens, frozen banana, plant milk, 1 tbsp nut butter, chia seeds. Vitamix makes it silky even with tough greens like kale.

Saturday Batch — 10 min

Tahini-Lemon Dressing (week supply)

Blend: ½ cup tahini, juice of 2 lemons, 1 garlic clove, pinch cumin, ½ cup water. Stores 7 days. Dresses salads, grain bowls, roasted veg.

Sneaky Nutrition — 5 min

Blended Greens into Sauces

Add a handful of spinach to any blended sauce — you won't taste it but you get the full nutrition. Works in the miso sauce, tahini dressing, or tomato-based sauces.
